Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how My Japanese Name Translator ("we," "us," or "our"), owned and operated by Daniel Sato, collects, uses, and protects information when you visit myjapanesenametranslator.com (the "Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using our Website, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Website.

Contact for Privacy Inquiries: Daniel Sato — contact@myjapanesenametranslator.com

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Our Japanese name translation tool processes the names you enter directly in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit the names you type into the converter. All name conversions happen client-side (in your browser) and are not sent to any server.

We do not currently collect personal information such as email addresses, account details, or user profiles. In the future, we may offer optional email subscriptions or contact forms — if so, we will update this policy and obtain your explicit consent before collecting any personal data.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Website, certain information may be collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level)

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Pages viewed and navigation paths
  • Session duration and bounce rate
  • Traffic sources (search engines, direct, referral)
  • Device and browser information
  • Approximate geographic location

Google Analytics data is processed by Google LLC. We have enabled IP anonymization where available. For more information about how Google processes data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies for the following purposes:

GDPR Compliance (European Union Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Your Rights

  • Right of Access — Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Request that we limit the processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability — Request your personal data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object — Object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent

Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate Interest — Website analytics to improve user experience
  • Consent — Cookies placed after user consent where required

To exercise any of your GDPR rights, please contact us at contact@myjapanesenametranslator.com. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

CCPA Compliance (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Delete — Request deletion of personal data we have collected
  • Right to Opt-Out — Opt out of the sale of your personal data (we do not sell personal data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — You will not be discriminated against for exercising your CCPA rights

As we are based in Stockton, California, we take our obligations under California law seriously. To exercise your CCPA rights, please email contact@myjapanesenametranslator.com.
